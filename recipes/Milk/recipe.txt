Advanced milk recipe:
1. Heat the milk:
Pour milk in a saucepan and place it over medium heat. Warm it but do not let it boil. Stir occasoinally prevent scorching.

2.Add Flavorings:
Once the milk is wam add a teaspoon of honey and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. 
Stir to well combined.

3. Add more spices:
Add a pinch of cinnomon and nutmeg to your liking and stir to well combined.

4. Add whipped cream
Pour your milk into a glass and add sugared whipped cream ontop and enjoy.
